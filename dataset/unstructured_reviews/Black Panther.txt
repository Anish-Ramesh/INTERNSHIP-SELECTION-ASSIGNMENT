movie: Black Panther
review: The 2018 film Black Panther is widely regarded by critics as a landmark achievement in the superhero genre. It holds a 97% "Fresh" rating on Rotten Tomatoes and was the first superhero film to be nominated for Best Picture at the Academy Awards.
Critical Consensus
Reviewers praised the film for moving beyond standard Marvel tropes to deliver a deeply personal family saga and political drama.

Direction and Vision: Critics from The New York Times and Variety lauded director Ryan Coogler for infusing the Marvel template with an "auteurist twist".

Standout Performances: Chadwick Boseman was celebrated for his stoic and regal portrayal of T’Challa, while Michael B. Jordan is frequently cited as one of the MCU's most compelling and sympathetic villains.

Cultural Significance: The film’s "Afrofuturist" aesthetic was hailed as revolutionary. Its portrayal of Wakanda as an uncolonised, technologically advanced African nation challenged decades of cinematic stereotypes.
